Who Are We?

We are an enterprise-grade digital asset escrow community designed to protect retail buyers and high-volume sellers. Founded by seasoned trade validators, G2G Marketplace acts as a legal and mechanical buffer between trading counter-parties across global server networks.

What We Do In Trading

When two individuals want to exchange items, cash, keys, or profile accounts, they open a secure desk ticket with us. The seller hands the asset securely to our certified middlemen. We run verification sweeps on the asset. Once the buyer completes their external financial transfer to the seller, we immediately override properties and release the trade parameters. No exit scams. No fake invoices. Absolute safety.
